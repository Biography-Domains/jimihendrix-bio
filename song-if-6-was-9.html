If 6 Was 9

From Axis: Bold as Love (1967)

History

A defiant counterculture statement featuring Hendrix's most explicit critique of conformity. Notable for guest appearances by Graham Nash and Gary Leeds on recorder. Later became iconic when used in Easy Rider (1969), becoming synonymous with 1960s rebellion.

Awards & Recognition
