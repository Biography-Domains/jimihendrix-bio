GUITARIST, SINGER-SONGWRITER, LEGEND

Jimi Hendrix

1942 - 1970

Widely regarded as the greatest electric guitarist in history, Jimi Hendrix revolutionized rock music in just four years. His innovative techniques, otherworldly sound, and transcendent performances redefined what was possible with the electric guitar, influencing every guitarist who followed.
