LEGENDARY CONCERTS

Live Performances

Witness the power of Hendrix on stage

Monterey Pop Festival

June 18, 1967 - Monterey, California

Hendrix's American debut performance that made him a star. Ended with the famous guitar-burning spectacle.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Wild Thing (Guitar Burning)
  • Hey Joe
  • Like a Rolling Stone
  • Rock Me Baby

Woodstock Festival

August 18, 1969 - Bethel, New York

The legendary Monday morning set including the iconic Star-Spangled Banner performance.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Star Spangled Banner
  • Purple Haze (Woodstock)
  • Voodoo Child (Slight Return)
  • Izabella
  • Fire (Woodstock)

Isle of Wight Festival

August 30, 1970 - Isle of Wight, England

One of Hendrix's final performances, just weeks before his death.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Machine Gun (Isle of Wight)
  • Red House (Isle of Wight)
  • Freedom (Isle of Wight)
  • All Along the Watchtower (Isle of Wight)

Fillmore East (Band of Gypsys)

January 1, 1970 - New York City

Historic New Year's shows with Band of Gypsys, featuring the definitive Machine Gun.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Machine Gun (Fillmore)
  • Who Knows
  • Power to Love
  • Stone Free (Fillmore)

Atlanta Pop Festival

July 4, 1970 - Atlanta, Georgia

One of Hendrix's largest audiences - over 500,000 people.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Stone Free (Atlanta)
  • Star Spangled Banner (Atlanta)
  • Straight Ahead (Atlanta)

Royal Albert Hall

February 24, 1969 - London, England

Classic Experience lineup in one of London's most prestigious venues.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Little Wing (Royal Albert Hall)
  • Voodoo Child (Slight Return) RAH
  • Bleeding Heart (RAH)

Berkeley Community Theatre

May 30, 1970 - Berkeley, California

Intense performances captured on film for the Jimi Plays Berkeley documentary.

SETLIST HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Johnny B. Goode (Berkeley)
  • Hey Baby (New Rising Sun)
  • Voodoo Child (Slight Return) Berkeley
