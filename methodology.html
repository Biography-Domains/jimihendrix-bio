Disclosures

Methodology: This biography relies heavily on archival documents, interviews, and secondary sources. Some personal details and private conversations have been reconstructed from available accounts and may not represent verbatim accuracy.
Uncertainty: Certain aspects of Hendrix's death remain subject to ongoing speculation and conspiracy theories. This biography presents the official coroner's findings while acknowledging areas of continuing debate among researchers.
Limitations: The Toronto arrest incident involved complex legal proceedings. While court records provide factual basis for events, some details of the circumstances remain disputed in various accounts.
Sources: Primary sources include official documents, broadcast recordings, and court records. Secondary sources have been cross-referenced where possible, though some biographical details rely on single contemporary accounts.
