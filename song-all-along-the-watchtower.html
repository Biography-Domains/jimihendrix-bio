All Along the Watchtower

From Electric Ladyland (1968)

History

Hendrix's interpretation of Dylan's song became so definitive that Dylan himself adopted Hendrix's arrangement for his own performances. Features multiple guitar overdubs (at least 12 recorded), creating a wall of sound. Dave Mason contributed acoustic twelve-string guitar. Dylan later said, 'It overwhelmed me, really. He had such talent.'
